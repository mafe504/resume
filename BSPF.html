The Operating Environment

Leading Through Geopolitical & Spatial Crisis

Reversing urban "educational flight" required transforming the school from a constrained historic site into an inviolable Psychological Safe Haven amidst extreme external friction.

The Geopolitical Reality

  • Located in the epicenter of the historic Old City of Jerusalem, navigating perpetual security tensions and heavy military presence.
  • Daily commutes involve checkpoints and invasive searches, instilling chronic traumatic stress in students.
  • Systemic marginalization and high poverty leading to severe "educational flight" to perceived safety in suburbs.

The Architectural Reality

  • Housed in a converted Ottoman-era residential building, restricted to a mere 407 square meters of floor space.
  • Rigid historic preservation laws strictly prohibit external expansion or new construction.
  • Zero traditional outdoor playground or recreational facilities due to the domestic, historic scaling.
